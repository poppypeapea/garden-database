Insert Value into Watering

For insertion, you must input a new wateringId that does not exist in WateringR2. Additionally, you must enter a unique {pH, temperature, and wateringDate} combination that does not already exist in WateringR1. Lastly, for plantId, it must be a plantId that exists in the plant table.

Delete Value from Watering R1

For deletion, you must enter a combination tuple for {wateringDate, temperature, pH} that exists in the WateringR1 table.

Update Tuple in Watering R2

For updating, you must enter a wateringId that exists in the wateringR2 table. Additionally, for the {wateringDate, temperature, pH} tuple combination, it must be a tuple that exists in the WateringR1 when updating. For the plantId, it must be a plantId that exists in the plant table.
